Mission Statement

>

The Air New Zealand Group operates a global network that provides air passenger services and cargo transport services

to, from and within New Zealand to more than 15 million passengers a year.

Logo Design

Air New Zealand uses the iconic koru symbol for most of its imagery and logo. The koru symbol is displayed on the tails of their planes, logo and on the uniforms of employees.

The silver fern is also used as imagery, around the airports and on the planes.
